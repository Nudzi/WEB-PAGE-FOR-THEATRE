KURDISH LULLABYE
08 Apr 2020

DIO ATMOSFERE SA ONLINE PROBE eMTeeM-a! :)
*If you want to read this in English scroll down*
Nastavljamo raditi na našoj novoj predstavi 𝗚𝗔𝗠𝗘 koja će javno progovoriti o životu i nevoljama 𝗺𝗶𝗴𝗿𝗮𝗻𝗮𝘁𝗮! (Koristimo materijale koje smo prikupili na 15-dnevnim radionicama sa grupama koje borave u Izbjegličkom kampu Salakovac - u saradnji sa LDA Mostar, a finansirane od strane EU u sklopu Erasmus+ programa) Tim povodom smo istražili muzičku kulturu ovih naroda i, pošto su bili u većini na radionicama, odabrali prelijepu Kurdsku uspavanku. Zove se 𝑳𝒂𝒚 𝒍𝒂𝒚 (lezi, lezi). Težak posao je bio koliko-toliko savladati izgovor i naučiti riječi, a zatim smo ostali zatečeni nakon što smo, što smo približnije mogli, preveli ovu dječiju 𝘂𝘀𝗽𝗮𝘃𝗮𝗻𝗸𝘂!
Prijevod glasi:
"Lezi.
Niko ne živi zauvijek.
Pa ni ja.
Budi pažljiv.
Lezi, slatko moje, budi dobar prema životu. Baci kockice, pogledaj svoju ulogu
i zapitaj se šta mogu uraditi za sebe.
Šta ja mogu uraditi?
Lezi, budi sretan zauvijek.
Molim te.
Iako je to skoro nemoguće.
Ponekad želim da ispustim dušu, ali uzmem zamah i zapitam se šta ja to mogu uraditi."

Čak i njihove uspavanke satkane su od boli i stalnog opreza. Ovo svjedoči o tome koliko je važno dijete upoznati sa svijetom na koji je došao. Da je okrutan i zao, ali da se uvijek trebamo zapitati "šta ja to mogu uraditi?"

A PART OF THE ATMOSPHERE FROM OUR ONLINE REHEARSAL

We continue to work on our new play called 𝗚𝗔𝗠𝗘. With this play we are hoping to have a chance to publicly speak about difficulties 𝗿𝗲𝗳𝘂𝗴𝗲𝗲𝘀 have to deal with in their lives! (We are using data we collected by doing several workshops with groups of refugees that are currently staying in the Salakovac Refugee center - in collaboration with LDA Mostar, funded by EU within the Erasmus+ programme) On this occasion we decided to explore their music and culture, and, because they were in the majority on the workshops, we chose a beautiful Kurdish 𝗹𝘂𝗹𝗹𝗮𝗯𝘆𝗲. It's called 𝑳𝒂𝒚 𝒍𝒂𝒚 (lay down) It wasn't easy at all to learn how to pronounce words of their language. But then, when we translated the lyrics, we were very surprised and confused.
Translation:
"Lay down.
No one lives forever.
Neither do I.
Be careful.
Lay down, my little sweetie, be kind to life.
Roll the dice, look at your role
and ask yourself 'what can I do for myself?'
What can I do?
Lay down and be happy forever.
Please.
Even though it's almost impossible.
Sometimes I wish to be taken and breathe my last, but I take a swing and ask myself "what can I do?' "

Even their lullabyes for children are full of pain and constant caution. This testifies about how it is important to explain our children the world they came into. How evil and cruel it is, but we can always ask ourselves "What can I do about it?"
